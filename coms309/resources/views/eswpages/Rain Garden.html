Rain Garden Project

Purpose: Rain gardens are an inﬁltration-based stormwater management practice that relies on soils with good percolation rates to help manage rainfall to protect water quality.


Benefits: Can provide a habitat for wildlife, aesthetically pleasing, more maintainable than lawns and reduce storm drain and flooding.
